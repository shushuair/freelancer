Costa Rica: The Land of Beauty and Abundance

Luxury Spas and Resorts

Try the award-winning Tabacon Grand Spa Thermal Resort sits in the middle of a picturesque rainforest, located in La Fortuna de San Carlos in the Arenal Volcano National Park. In addition to five-star accommodations, meals and spa treatments, Tabacon also provides private jungle tours, hikes and river rafting for the luxury adventurer. With a view of the spectacular Arenal volcano, Tabacon Grand Spa Thermal Resort is the best way to enjoy the grandeur of nature in the height of luxury.

Explore The Natural Beauty

Costa Rica is one of the most bio-diverse countries in the world, with its eco-tourism garnering much deserved attention. With tours ranging from family friendly to private guides, there are many ways to be immersed in the captivating wonders that Costa Rica has to offer. Draped in mist and painted in hues of green, the enchanting cloudy rainforest of Monteverde is an inspiring site for people of all ages. There are also many guided animal tours available for those who wish to see a diverse array of wildlife.

Manuel Antonio National Park is the smallest national park in Costa Rica. With a dense concentration of wildlife, spotting animals as you stroll through the verdant wilderness is an exciting experience for nature enthusiasts. If you wish to see the beauties of the island from afar, luxury HeliJets are available for chartering and offer leather interiors, air conditioning, music, beverages, and snacks. Seating up to 6 passengers, HeliJet tours are a great opportunity for families, groups or couples to see the country in luxury and privacy.

The Caribbean side of Costa Rica offers calm, aquamarine waters, perfect for relaxing and lounging in, as well as various water activities. The Pacific coast is world-renowned for its waves, attracting surfing enthusiasts from all over the globe. Many Costa Rican beaches occur where ocean meets jungle, creating a breathtaking juxtaposition of the country's two most picturesque landscapes.
